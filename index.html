About Me

Hi, I'm Gillian Elizabeth Young, a dedicated student currently studying Global Business and Digital Arts with an Economics minor at the University of Waterloo. My interests lie both in business innovation and digital design, and I am actively exploring how I'd like to merge these fields in real-world applications.

I am excited to continue to learn how I can bring a blend of creativity and strategic thinking into everything I do. For more information, check out my program at the University of Waterloo Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business.
